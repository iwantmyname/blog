Timely Reminder: Domain Customise Your Calendar

It’s been quite a year for the founders of Kiwi tech start-up Timely, a cloud hosted calendar and scheduling system for small business. They not only aced the BNZ Startup Alley at Webstock and received accolades from the media. They also secured a “timely” investment round from wily tech entrepreneur Rowan Simpson.

Timely got up and running last year using the Windows Azure hosting platform and hasn’t looked back since. Timely looks good and integrates nicely with Xero and Vend plus all your favourite Windows and Apple time management tools. The nifty app also posts event reminders and handles staff rostering.

We love supporting cool Kiwi start-ups, so we set up an integration of our own and added Timely to our apps marketplace. Remember, iwantmyname makes it one-click easy to add the correct DNS records to your domain. Now find out how you can domain customise your Timely set-up.
