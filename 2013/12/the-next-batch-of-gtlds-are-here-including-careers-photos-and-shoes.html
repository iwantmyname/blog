The Next Batch Of gTLDs Are Here, Including .CAREERS, .PHOTOS And .RECIPES

With 2014 quickly approaching, ICANN has been busy delegating generic Top-Level Domains (gTLDs). Just this week, 17 new gTLDs have been delegated--the most we've seen in a single batch yet. And as we get into the new year, we'll be seeing more and more delegations. But let's not get impatient. This new batch of gTLDs has a lot to offer.

We have .CAREERS, .COMPANY and .MANAGEMENT for business-minded folks. .CAB and .LIMO for getting around town. .ACADEMY, .CAMP and .CENTER for education. .COMPUTER, .DOMAINS and .SYSTEMS for tech. And .PHOTOS, .RECIPES and .SHOES for just about everyone else. We even have some great foreign language options in .VIAJES and .公益.

Once specific launch dates are announced, we'll definitely let you know--but don't forget that you can pre-order them now to get your name on the list. Also, check our our newly updated gTLD page, with pre-order links to all 481 domain extensions we have listed.
