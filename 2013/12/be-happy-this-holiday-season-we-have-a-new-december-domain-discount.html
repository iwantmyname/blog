.BE Happy This Holiday Season, We Have A New December Domain Discount

It's the final month of the year, and we have some great domain discounts to pass along. Some that carry over from November, but one great new one--.BE.

.BE is the domain extension for Belgium, and is perfect for businesses in the European country, as well as websites that want a clever domain hack (maybe something like iwantto.be?). For the month of December, .BE domains are on sale for $14.90 USD, 21% off their normal price.

There are a number of other domain extensions for sale, including .IO, .ME and .CC, so check out our Special Offers page for the full rundown. And be sure to check back at the beginning of each month for great new updates.
