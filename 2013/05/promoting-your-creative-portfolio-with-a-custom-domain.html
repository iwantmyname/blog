Promoting Your Creative Portfolio With A Custom Domain

At iwantmyname we have a growing following amongst the creative community who love the idea of customising an online portfolio site with their chosen domain name. Once you purchase a domain, it is a simple step to select the required hosted portfolio app and click to automagically add the correct DNS settings on the domain. Voila!
