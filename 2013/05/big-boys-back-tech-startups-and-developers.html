Big Boys Back Tech Startups And Developers

At iwantmyname we’ve been longtime fans and supporters of Startup Weekends in New Zealand. Last weekend’s Auckland event was no exception and we were pleased to shout some drinks for thirsty punters who rocked up to the Viaduct Basin on Friday night ready to do battle. Lenz and Paul from the iwantmyname team also took time out to travel north and get involved.

But there’s a whole bunch of other sponsor companies on board that help ensure Startup Weekends keep popping up like mushrooms. We’d like to make special mention of Microsoft NZ and BizSpark who have really got behind the start-up scene in New Zealand lately and are big supporters of Startup Weekend. Thiago Almeida (pictured) has been leading the charge by connecting with the tech community.

These days, starter uppers have a diverse range of resources and useful low cost tools on offer such as Microsoft’s Windows Azure Websites to get their start-up projects underway. Developers are certainly smart enough to know what the appropriate gear is for the kinds of projects that interest them, so recent hand-wringing about vendor lock-in seem a little overstated. Like the domain registrar industry, there’s no shortage of competition out there. The bottom line is service quality and stability plus capability to scale.

Well done Arrive Supplied who scooped the pool this time around with their bespoke care package service for travellers.
