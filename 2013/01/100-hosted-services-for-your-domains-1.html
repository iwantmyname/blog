100+ Hosted Services For Your Domains

Since launching iwantmyname, our mission has been to help you find and set up the best hosted services through our unique app marketplace for domains. The number of listings is constantly growing and now features more than 100 applications with easy 1-click DNS setup.

The iwantmyname marketplace lets you discover hosting services and apps from 15 different categories:

  • Blog: Blogetery, Blogger, Jux, Page.ly, Posterous, Scriptogram, Soup.io, Tumblr, TypePad
  • Business: Google Apps, Desk.com, Get Satisfaction, Onepager, Tender Support, Uservoice, Zendesk
  • Developer: Amazon S3, App Engine, AppFog, Bitbucket, cloudControl, dotCloud, Github Pages, Heroku, Pingdom, Windows Azure
  • Ecommerce: Big Cartel, Enstore, Flying Cart, Goodsie, Shopify, Storenvy, SupaDupa, Tictail
  • Email: Gmail (Google Apps), Fastmail.fm, Rackspace Email, Windows Live, Zoho Mail
  • Forum: Vanilla Forums, Zoho Discussions
  • Mobile: Atmio, DudaMobile
  • Music: Bandcamp, Onesheet, Soundfolder
  • Open Source: Drupal Gardens, Magento Go, WP Engine
  • Personal: DooID, Flavors.me, Forrst, Zerply
  • Portfolio: 22slides, 4ormat, 500px, Behance Prosite, Carbonmade, Cargo, DeviantART, Parade, PortfolioDeck, Projeqt, Salon.io, SmugMug, Viewbook, Zenfolio
  • Social: DonationTo, Grou.ps, KickApps, Meetup, Ning, Twylah
  • URL Shortener: awe.sm, bit.ly, CloudApp, Droplr, ShortSwitch
  • Website Builder: Breezi, DropPages, Harmony, Jimdo, KickoffLabs, Launchrock, LetsEat, LightCMS, Moonfruit, Pagebuild, Site44, Squarespace, VidCaster, Vimeo, Virb, Weebly, Wix, YikeSite, Yola, Zoho Sites
  • Wiki: PBworks, Wikidot, Zoho Wiki

As an iwantmyname customer, you will be able to add the DNS settings for any of these services with just one single click from the dashboard, e.g. create a website with Virb for yourdomain.com, start an additional shop through Shopify at shop.yourdomain.com, set up a knowledgbase on support.yourdomain.com with Desk.com and many more (some of them even offer free hosting). It's really the easiest way to get started online!
