Apps For Domains News Roundup February

Here's the latest news from the services in our iWantMyName marketplace which crossed the number of fifty domain-brandable applications this month:

  • Blogger: Introducing Blogger Android App

  • Gmail: Advanced sign-in security for your Google account

  • Google Apps: New Integrated Web Apps For Your Domain

  • Ning: Integrates with music service SoundCloud

  • Posterous: Adds five more beautiful themes and enables multiple admins for blogs

  • Tumblr: Now available in Italian and French

  • Weebly: Introduces Web Fonts and Social Media Buttons

  • Windows Azure: Introductory Special - Includes 750 Free Hours of Windows Azure Extra-Small Instance and More

There also were some new additions to the iWantMyName marketplace with DNS templates for Amazon S3 (static website hosting), DuoStack (node.js & Ruby cloud hosting), Forrst (personal profile), WP Engine, page.ly (premium WordPress hosting), PHP Fog (PHP cloud platform) and YikeSite (whitelabel CMS). All these services can now be easily added to your domains without any technical knowledge required.
