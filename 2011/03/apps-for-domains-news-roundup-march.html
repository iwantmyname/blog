Apps for Domains News Roundup March

Read the latest buzz from the services in our iWantMyName marketplace which helps you discover and set up the best applications for your domain names:

  • Big Cartel introduces built-in Google Analytics and asks the question "Big Cartel or Etsy?".

  • bit.ly has a brand new cross-platform mobile site for creating and managing short links on the go.

  • Blogger gets new dynamic template designs and lets readers follow your blog by email.

  • Gmail makes switching email accounts easier and adds more international services to their list of supported providers.

  • Google App Engine announces App Engine 1.4.3 release to bring Java and Python runtimes closer to parity.

  • Shopify releases an official Shopify theme especially for fashion shops.

  • Viewbook launches a new techblog worth checking out and new mobile galleries for iPad, iPhone and Android.

  • Zoho Discussions now offers private communities.

We also have some new additions to the iWantMyName marketplace, namely BandCamp, DropPages, Enstore, LaunchRock, Nodester, SoundFolder and PregoApp. All these web services can now be added to your domains in just one single click.
