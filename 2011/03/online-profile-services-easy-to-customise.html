Online Profile Services Easy To Customise

At iWantMyName we make it really easy to personalise your web profile service with a domain of your choice. A number of the big social networking sites don't allow this, so where do you go if you want a profile site that reflects your personality? We've dug up some cool web profile services that we think you'll love.

First up we are big Tumblr fans and have to say it makes a great profile service as well as a blogging site. It's really easy to tailor your Tumblr site with nice images or backgrounds and posting is a breeze too. Flavors.me is also a great way to go, but differs in that it aggregates all your content in one place. Flavors.me is especially handy if you have a creative business and want to show off your talents.

A personal domain anchors and protects your presence online and best of all its portable. There's a bunch of other services on our profile services page that you can have fun exploring as well.
