Small businesses without a web presence are missing out

Canadians spend more time online than any other country—45.6 hours per month—according to CIRA. So it’s not surprising that three-quarters of Canadians research purchases online.

What is surprising, then, is that as this article states, only 45.5 percent of Canadian small businesses are online. (41 percent have a website, specifically.) It’s safe to say even fewer have a mobile-friendly web presence, despite the explosion in smartphone popularity and mobile usage. And despite having nearly ten times the population, the stats in the United States are similar.
