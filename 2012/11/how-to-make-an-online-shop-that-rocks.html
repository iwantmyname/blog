How To Make An Online Shop That Rocks

Lower Hutt web designer and rock fan Dave Keyes recently decided to combine his passions by launching an e-commerce website to distribute rock themed clothing and gifts. Part of the profits go to the Cancer Society of New Zealand, so we figured thisisatribute.com was the perfect candidate for our November website of the month.

Dave recently became a Dad, so it's no surprise that designer-wear for junior rockers are featured prominently on the site. We think the "Red Hot Chilli" jump suits are cute as a button, so if you have fashionable friends with newborns, you should definitely point them in this direction. Prints, badges and tee shirts are also available.

The site was built using hosted online shop builder Shopify. iwantmyname makes it easy to customise a Shopify web store front with your own domain. Setting up a simple online sales site is easy these days and a great, low cost way to test the market with a new product line.
