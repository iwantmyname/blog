Short Domain Search Made Easy!

One of the nice things about our new iwantmyname search is the ability to check domain availability for ultra-short domains. If you enter a 2-letter domain, we will automatically display only search results for top-level domains (TLDs) allowing registration of these little gems:

The current list of country code TLDs supporting domains consisting of two characters is:

.AF, .AG, .CX, .FM, .GD, .GL, .GS, .GY, .HT, .IO, .MS, .MU, .PE, .TL, .VG

Short domains are a rare commodity, for example there are less than 100 letter-only 2-char .IO domains left! Grab a tiny web address for your app or URL shortener and start your short domain search now.
