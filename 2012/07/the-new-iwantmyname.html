The Brand New iwantmyname

We're happy to announce the launch of our site redesign and new iwantmyname logo. As mentioned last week this refresh is a first step towards a new control panel which will speed up the interaction with your domains dramatically. We can't wait to share it with you!

What has changed?

  • A new logo designed by New Zealand based designer Jarred Bishop who you also might know as the developer of several awesome Tumblr themes.
  • Faster domain search with all 100+ domain extensions on one single page.
  • The layout has been moved to Twitter's Bootstrap framework, customized to our needs.
  • Small improvements to our marketplace and iwantmyname dashboard, mainly a new internal navigation.

We'd also like to thank Wellington developers bit by bit who have been working with us on the new site. And now what are you waiting for? Check out the new site and domain search!
