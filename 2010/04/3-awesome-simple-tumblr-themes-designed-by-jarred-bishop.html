3 Awesomely Simple Tumblr Themes Designed by Jarred Bishop

It's not often that you meet talented people with an eye for real simple designs. We were lucky to catch up with one of them last week. Local designer Jarred Bishop developed three custom Tumblr themes and his "Wordographic" theme was selected by Tumblr as one of the few templates available in their new premium theme garden. Jarred also told us that he's got other themes cooking so stay tuned for more excellent customization options (besides your own domain name) for your blog or portfolio.
