Tumblr + Gmail: How To Easily Customize Your Page And Email With An Own Domain

It's dead easy to set up Tumblr and Gmail on a custom domain name with iWantMyName. If you register a domain with us, you do not need any geeky knowledge to get the customized version of both the popular blog and email service up and running. Moreover, it's completely free to host your domain on Tumblr and Google Apps and hence the perfect combination to start owning your online identity.

Simply follow these 5 steps to personalize your experience of the web:

  1. Search for an available domain name. Most popular for personal sites are .COM, .NET, .ORG, .ME (on sale!), .NAME and .IM domains or your preferred country code extension.
  2. Register the domain and proceed to the "Services" tab in your iWantMyName dashboard.
  3. Choose "Google Apps" to set up your email following these instructions.
  4. Add the domain to your Tumblr account.
  5. Go to "Tumblr" in the list of services at iWantMyName and click on "Set up Tumblr".

That's it! You should now see your Tumblr blog on your shiny new domain name and also be able to send and receive emails with Gmail @yourdomain.com/net/org. Benefit from the world's easiest custom domain setup for the most popular web services and register or transfer your domain name today!
