Big week for new domains, including .WIKI, .NINJA and .FLIGHTS

May has been a relatively slow month for generic Top-Level Domains (at least by quantity… .CLUB has been huge), but this last week is a big one. Over the course of two days, we have eight gTLDs coming out, with a couple of them sure to hover around the top 20 in popularity. We have:

The week for travel TLDs

Travel planning is a major industry on the web, and this week .CRUISES, .FLIGHTS, .RENTALS, .VACATIONS and .VILLAS are all going live on the same day.
