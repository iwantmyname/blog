Meet the domain extensions of December, including .Paris, .pizza, and .business

December is a bit of a slow month for new generic top-level domains (gTLDs), but the lack of quantity doesn't mean there's a lack of quality. This month, we have a big city launch in .Paris, a surefire hit in the pizza world with .pizza, and some additional TLDs that could be big in business circles.

Also, with this being the last month of the year, be sure to check out how far the gTLD program has come. In the past year, we've added 247 new domain extensions to our collection, and will be above the 250 mark by the end of the month.
